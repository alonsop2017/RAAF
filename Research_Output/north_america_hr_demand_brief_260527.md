## Key Cross-Border Dynamics

**1. US firms poaching Canadian talent via USD compensation**
Every lawyer, CPA, and AI engineer who gets poached creates a vacancy for your client. This is repeat business built into the market structure.

**2. US companies opening Canadian offices — most fee-tolerant clients**
Companies like Salesforce, Microsoft, Amazon, and Google use Toronto/Vancouver as satellite offices. They have no Canadian HR infrastructure and will pay premium fees. Canada's Global Talent Stream (2-week work permit for tech roles) makes this structurally attractive.

**3. H-1B holders looking at Canada**
Since the second Trump term, H-1B renewal uncertainty has created a pipeline of US-trained professionals exploring Canadian employer-sponsored pathways. A firm bridging US-trained talent into Canadian or US-company Canadian-office roles has unique positioning no domestic-only firm can match.
