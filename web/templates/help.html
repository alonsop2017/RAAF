How It Works

Workflows, scoring framework, and recommendation tiers

Supported Workflows

RAAF supports three distinct workflows. Choose the one that matches how candidates reach you. The full combined diagram is available as a downloadable PNG.

Workflow A — PCR-Integrated Recommended

Candidates apply via Indeed, flow automatically into PCRecruiter, and RAAF pulls them with one click.

Figure 1A — PCR-Integrated workflow (end-to-end)

  1. Create position in PCRecruiter (Job Code: INDML)
  2. PCR posts the job to Indeed automatically
  3. Candidates apply → resumes stored in PCR
  4. Click PCR Sync in RAAF to pull applicants
  5. Click Download Resumes to retrieve files
  6. Extract text & create a batch
  7. Run AI Assessment — all candidates in one click
  8. Review & refine scores
  9. Generate client report (DOCX)
  10. Push scores back to PCR
  11. Update PCR pipeline status
  12. Send personalized interview invitations
  13. Deliver report to client
  14. Archive requisition when filled

Workflow B — Manual Upload

Resumes arrive by email or direct download. Upload them straight into RAAF.

Figure 1B — Manual / Direct Upload workflow

  1. Receive JD from client (PDF or DOCX)
  2. Initialise client & requisition in RAAF
  3. Upload JD → AI generates assessment framework
  4. Drag-and-drop resumes (PDF / DOCX)
  5. Extract text & create a batch
  6. Run AI Assessment
  7. Review & refine scores
  8. Generate client report (DOCX)
  9. Send personalized interview invitations
  10. Deliver report to client
  11. Archive requisition when filled

Workflow C — Repository Search

Every assessed candidate is stored. Search past talent before starting a new search from scratch.

Figure 1C — Candidate Repository Search workflow

  1. New position opens (same or new client)
  2. Open Search in the top navigation
  3. Upload JD for AI matching or search by keyword
  4. AI ranks past candidates by fit to new role
  5. Review previous assessment scores & evidence
  6. Reach out directly to warm candidates
  7. Add to new requisition & re-assess if needed
  8. Continue via Workflow A or B from assessment onward

AI Scoring Framework

Every candidate is scored across six weighted categories. Each score is backed by direct evidence from the resume — no guesswork.

Core Experience & Qualifications25%
Technical & Analytical Skills20%
Communication & Relationships20%
Strategic & Business Acumen15%
Job Stability10%
Cultural Fit & Soft Skills10%

Recommendation Tiers

Scores map directly to a hiring recommendation. Thresholds can be adjusted per requisition in the framework settings.

TierScoreAction
STRONG RECOMMEND85%+Advance immediately
RECOMMEND70–84%Advance to interview
CONDITIONAL55–69%Hold in reserve
DO NOT RECOMMEND<55%Do not advance
