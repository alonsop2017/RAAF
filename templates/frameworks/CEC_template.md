## Customization Notes

This framework has been adapted from the base template with the following modifications:

1. **Category weights adjusted** to reflect CEC role priorities:
   - Canvas Competency Assessment: 45 points (53%)
   - Required Experience Assessment: 35 points (41%)
   - Job Stability Assessment: 5 points (6%)

2. **Scoring scale modified** from 0-10 to 0-5 per criterion to match role-specific granularity requirements

3. **Nine Canvas Competencies** added to capture customer experience marketing focus

4. **Seven Required Experience criteria** aligned with SaaS/technology marketing environment

5. **Recommendation thresholds** adjusted to 85-point scale

## Usage Instructions

1. Read the candidate's resume thoroughly
2. Score each criterion (0-5) based on evidence found in resume/application
3. Document specific evidence for each score in the "Evidence / Notes" column
4. Calculate category subtotals and overall total
5. Calculate percentage (Raw Score ÷ 85 × 100)
6. Apply recommendation threshold
7. Note key strengths, concerns, and interview focus areas
8. Sign and date the assessment
