## Key Indicators for Construction PM Roles

### Strong Positive Signals
- Projects delivered on time and under budget
- Progressive responsibility growth
- PMP or relevant certifications
- Strong safety record (zero LTI)
- Experience with similar project types
- Technology adoption (Procore, BIM)
- Owner/client relationship management

### Red Flags
- Safety incidents or violations
- Budget overruns without explanation
- Litigation involvement
- No verifiable project completions
- Resistance to technology
- Poor subcontractor relationships

### Interview Focus Areas
- Walk through a challenging project (scope, budget, schedule)
- How they handle change orders and scope creep
- Safety incident response and prevention
- Subcontractor dispute resolution
- Technology tools and reporting approach

## Project Type Specializations

Consider alignment with these common specializations:

- **Commercial:** Office buildings, retail, hospitality
- **Industrial:** Manufacturing, warehouses, distribution
- **Healthcare:** Hospitals, medical facilities
- **Educational:** Schools, universities
- **Residential (Multi-family):** Apartments, condos
- **Infrastructure:** Roads, bridges, utilities
- **Renovation/TI:** Tenant improvements, retrofits
