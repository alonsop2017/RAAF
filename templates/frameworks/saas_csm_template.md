## Key Indicators for SaaS CSM Roles

### Strong Positive Signals
- Quantified retention/NRR improvements
- Experience with enterprise customers ($500K+ ARR)
- Led executive business reviews
- Cross-functional influence (Product, Sales, Support)
- Gainsight/ChurnZero/Totango experience
- Technical aptitude (API understanding, integrations)

### Red Flags
- No quantified metrics/outcomes
- SMB-only experience for enterprise role
- Frequent job changes (<18 months average)
- No evidence of customer advocacy
- Purely reactive/support-focused experience

### Interview Focus Areas
- Specific examples of saving at-risk accounts
- Expansion/upsell conversation approach
- How they prioritize across a book of business
- Technical depth for the product category
- Executive presence assessment
