## Key Indicators for SaaS AE Roles

### Strong Positive Signals
- Documented quota attainment (W2 verifiable)
- Named enterprise logo wins
- Complex, multi-stakeholder deal examples
- Sales methodology certification
- President's Club / Top performer recognition
- Territory or vertical expertise alignment

### Red Flags
- No quantified quota performance
- Job hopping without progression
- SMB-only for enterprise role
- No methodology experience
- Blame-shifting language about missed targets
- No executive selling experience

### Interview Focus Areas
- Walk through a recent complex deal (MEDDIC framework)
- How they handle objections and competition
- Territory planning and pipeline management
- Collaboration with SE, CSM, leadership
- Verify quota attainment with documentation
